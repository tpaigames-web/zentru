Zentru Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Zentru ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal finance and credit card management application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app.

2. Data Collection

Zentru does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All your financial data is stored locally on your device only.

  • We do not have servers that receive your data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not collect crash reports automatically
  • We do not access your contacts, location, or other device data

3. Data Storage

All data including credit card information, transactions, budgets, and settings are stored locally on your device using IndexedDB (a browser/app-local database). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it using the backup feature.

4. Camera Permission

The app may request camera permission for the card scanning feature (OCR). Photos taken for card scanning are processed entirely on your device using local OCR technology (Tesseract.js) and are never uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

5. Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications for payment due date reminders. These notifications are generated and delivered locally on your device.

6. Data Export & Backup

You can export your data as CSV or JSON files. These files are saved to your device's storage. You are responsible for the security of exported data files.

7. Third-Party Services

Zentru does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

8. Children's Privacy

Zentru is not designed for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
